⚖ Art Thou · Buyer & Creator Protection

A Safe Space.
No Exceptions.

Art Thou was built so that real creators can thrive and real buyers can trust every purchase. We have zero tolerance for deception — from either side. This page explains exactly how we protect everyone.

✦ Buyer Protected
✦ Creator Protected
⊘ Scammers Banned
⊘ Fakes Removed
✦ 7-Day Review System
✦ Fair to Both Sides
Official Policy · Effective Immediately

Zero Tolerance.
Zero Exceptions.

Anyone who attempts to deceive a buyer — whether by misrepresenting a product, sending a fake, substituting a lesser item, or staging false evidence — faces suspension or permanent removal from Art Thou. Anyone who attempts to falsely accuse a creator, fabricate damage, or abuse the review system to obtain a free product faces the same consequences. Every decision is based on evidence, and honest mistakes can be appealed.

We are here to protect the honest. We have no interest in hosting the dishonest.

✦ The Art Thou 7-Day Protection System

How Every Purchase
Is Protected

From the moment your order arrives, the clock starts. Here is exactly what happens at every stage.

📦
📬
Item Arrives
Delivery confirmed. Your 7-day protection window opens immediately.
7 days
🔍
Inspect & Decide
You have the full 7 days to inspect your item and open a dispute if something's wrong — or do nothing and let it complete.
Step 1
📸

+48h

Review
👁
Art Thou Reviews
Our team examines all evidence from both sides independently and fairly.
Verdict
⚖️
Decision Made
A decision is issued, usually within 2–3 days of receiving all evidence. Both sides are notified.
Closed
✅
Case Closed

✦
✅
Outcome A — Creator Proven Right

Creator Is Vindicated. Paid Promptly.

If our team confirms the product was as described — the creator receives their full payment. The dispute is closed and the buyer's claim is denied.

⊘
🚫
Outcome B — Creator at Fault

Full Refund. Suspension or Removal.

If evidence confirms the creator misrepresented their product — the buyer receives a full refund. The creator faces suspension or removal from Art Thou, with a right to appeal under Section 07.

⊘
⛔
Outcome C — False Claim by Buyer

Claim Denied. Buyer Account Action.

If evidence shows the buyer fabricated damage or filed a false claim — the refund is denied and the creator is paid in full. The buyer faces suspension or removal, with a right to appeal under Section 07.

↩
↩️
Outcome D — Buyer Cancels Dispute

Buyer Withdraws. Creator Paid. Case Closed.

The buyer may cancel any time before a decision is issued. Once cancelled, the creator is paid and the transaction is permanently closed.

Section 01

Our Commitment to This Community

Art Thou was created to be a genuine, creative, and safe space for artists and art lovers across every country. We believe that when a buyer purchases a piece of art, they deserve to receive exactly what they were shown. We believe that when a creator pours their time, skill, and soul into a piece, they deserve to be paid fairly and protected from bad-faith claims.

This policy exists to uphold both of those beliefs equally. We do not take sides by default. We follow evidence.

Section 02

The 7-Day Buyer Protection Window

Upon confirmed delivery of any item purchased through Art Thou, the buyer enters a 7-day protection window.

  • The buyer has the full 7 days from confirmed delivery to inspect the item and open a dispute if it does not match what was listed.
  • Opening a dispute is entirely optional. If no dispute is opened within the 7 days, the transaction completes automatically and the creator is paid.
  • The buyer may cancel their dispute at any time before a decision is issued. Upon cancellation, the creator is paid and the case is permanently closed.
  • Both parties are notified at every stage with full transparency.
Section 03

What Constitutes a Valid Dispute

  • The item received is materially different from what was photographed or described in the listing.
  • The item is counterfeit or mass-produced when listed as an original handmade work.
  • The item arrived damaged beyond reasonable shipping wear and was not disclosed prior to purchase.
  • The creator deliberately misrepresented dimensions, materials, condition, or origin.
  • The item received is a completely different product than what was ordered.
Section 04

What Creators Must Provide as Evidence

When a dispute is filed, the creator has 48 hours to respond with supporting evidence:

  • Photographs or video of the creation process
  • Original listing photos compared to photos of the shipped item
  • Shipping receipts, packing photos, or video of the item being packaged
  • A signed certificate of authenticity or written statement of originality
  • Any third-party verification, gallery records, or provenance documentation

If the creator does not respond within 48 hours, the dispute is decided on the available evidence, which will generally favour the buyer and result in a refund.

Section 05 — Zero Tolerance

Fraud, Deception & Sabotage

The following are serious violations that may result in suspension or permanent removal from Art Thou, depending on the severity and the evidence:

  • Sending a product that is knowingly fake, counterfeit, or not the item shown in the listing.
  • Replacing the original item with a lesser item before shipping.
  • Fabricating process photos or falsifying a certificate of authenticity.
  • A buyer filing a false damage claim or attempting to keep an item while obtaining a refund.
  • Either party attempting to bribe, threaten, or coerce the other party or the Art Thou review team.
  • Any harassment directed at the other party during or after a dispute.
  • Creating a new account after a permanent ban. This will result in legal action where applicable.

Where our team finds clear evidence of deliberate fraud or sabotage from either side, the account may be removed. Confirmed fraud is not eligible for appeal; all other decisions may be appealed under Section 07.

Section 06

How Refunds Are Processed

If a refund is approved, it is issued to the buyer's original payment method. Refunds typically appear within 5–10 business days, though the exact timing depends on your bank or card provider and is outside Art Thou's control. If the creator is vindicated, their payment is released as quickly as possible once the case closes, subject to standard payment-processing times. Art Thou does not charge any additional fee for dispute resolution.

If a refund requires the item to be returned, the policy in Section 11 applies.

Section 07

Appeals & Final Decisions

Either party may submit a formal appeal within 48 hours of a decision if new evidence has come to light. Appeals are reviewed by a senior Art Thou team member separate from the original reviewer. Cases involving confirmed fraud are not eligible for appeal.

Section 08 — Open Offers House

Anti-Manipulation & Offer Integrity

Art Thou operates a competitive offer marketplace — not a licensed auction. All offer activity must be genuine. The following are strictly prohibited and may result in suspension or permanent removal:

  • A creator placing offers on their own listing directly or through any third party to artificially inflate the price. This is offer manipulation and is illegal in most jurisdictions.
  • Coordinating with friends, family, or other accounts to place fake competing offers on a listing.
  • Withdrawing a listing after genuine offers have been placed without a verifiable reason.
  • Misrepresenting an item as a 1-of-1 original when copies, prints, or duplicates exist.
  • All 1-of-1 listings must include proof of originality — process photos, a signed statement, or a certificate of authenticity — before going live.
  • Starting offer prices must be genuine. Hidden reserve prices are not permitted. What buyers see is what the offer opens at.
  • Art Thou holds all offer payments in escrow until the 7-day buyer protection window closes.
Section 09 — DMCA

Copyright & DMCA Takedown Policy

Art Thou respects intellectual property rights and complies fully with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act. If you believe any content on Art Thou infringes your copyright, you may submit a formal takedown request by contacting us at legal@artthou.com with the following information:

  • A description of the copyrighted work you believe has been infringed
  • The URL or location of the infringing content on Art Thou
  • Your contact information including name, address, and email
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized
  • A statement that the information in your notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on their behalf

Upon receiving a valid DMCA notice, Art Thou will remove the infringing content within 48 hours and notify the creator. Creators who repeatedly infringe copyrights will be permanently removed from the platform. Art Thou operates under the safe harbor provisions of the DMCA and is not liable for content uploaded by creators provided we act promptly upon receiving valid notices.

Section 10 — Shipping

Shipping Policy & Creator Obligations

Art Thou connects buyers directly with creators worldwide. Shipping is handled by the creator, but Art Thou sets clear standards to protect both sides.

  • Creators must ship within 5 business days of a confirmed sale. Failure to ship on time affects the creator's trust level and may result in order cancellation.
  • Tracking numbers are required for all orders over $50. Creators must provide the tracking number to the buyer within 24 hours of shipment.
  • Express shipping (DHL, FedEx, UPS) is available for 2–5 day worldwide delivery. The creator arranges and the cost is added at checkout.
  • Standard shipping uses national postal services and typically takes 7–21 days internationally.
  • Digital delivery is instant and free. Creators upload files directly and buyers download immediately after purchase.
  • Local pickup is free and arranged directly between buyer and creator where offered.
  • Lost packages — if a package is confirmed lost by the carrier, Art Thou holds the payment in escrow until the situation is resolved. The creator must provide proof of shipment. Genuine carrier loss does not penalize the creator.
  • Damaged items — if an item arrives damaged due to inadequate packaging by the creator, the buyer may open a dispute under the 7-day protection system. Creators are responsible for packaging fragile pieces appropriately.
  • Creators who provide false tracking numbers or claim to have shipped without doing so will be suspended or permanently removed.
Section 11 — Returns

Return Shipping on Refunds

Some refunds require the item to be sent back to the creator. Who pays return shipping depends on the reason for the refund:

  • If the refund is due to the creator's fault (item not as described, damaged through poor packaging, wrong item sent), the creator covers return shipping. The refund is completed once the item is shipped back with tracking.
  • If the refund is granted as a goodwill exception with no fault by either side, the buyer covers return shipping unless the creator agrees otherwise.
  • Buyers should not return an item until a decision instructs them to. Return tracking must be provided so the refund can be released.
Section 12 — Digital Items

Protection for Digital Products

Digital downloads (digital art, prints, templates, and similar files) work differently from physical items, since there is nothing to ship or return. The following applies to all digital purchases:

  • A buyer may open a dispute within 7 days of purchase if the file is corrupted, will not download, is unusable, or is materially different from what the listing showed.
  • Because digital files cannot be "returned," approved refunds are issued without a return, and the buyer's access to the file may be revoked.
  • Buyer's remorse — simply changing your mind about a working file that matches its description — is not grounds for a refund on digital items.
  • Reselling, redistributing, or claiming authorship of a creator's digital file is a serious violation and may result in removal.
